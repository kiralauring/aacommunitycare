Lets make some goals and hopes for ourselves and our Asian American communities and families! ☀

This is a community brainstorm, so contribute or browse with respect!

Oh hi,

Filial debt puts a lot of pressure on us to perform in capitalism and racist + ableist structures that are not good for us! What are some hopes and dreams you have for yourself and your Asian American family, communities, and friends?


Another thing...

Kai Cheng Thom has 2 life changing! articles about how to be face the fear of accountability for our own abusive behavior that furthers systems of harm and intergenerational violence. You can read them here and here . What are some ways that you want to be accountable, and how will you do this?


Something else!

What are some of your favorite readings/comics/movies/music/resources etc. that make you feel good?


You guessed it...another prompt :)

What is something you are really want to ask/question your family about but haven't? Maybe because you think they won't listen, will get mad, or are afraid to open up, etc.


Similarly...

What is a piece of advice you would give your parents if they would listen? Youth deserve autonomy and respect too! Tell them here what is on your mind.


One more! Make it count.
